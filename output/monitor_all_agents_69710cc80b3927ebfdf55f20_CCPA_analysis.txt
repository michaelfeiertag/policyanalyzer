*   **The Issue:** The agent moves raw conversation logs—potentially containing PII/SPII—from the secure Lindy environment into a **Google Sheet**.

**B. Violation of "Right to Delete" (Civil Code § 1798.105)**

*   **The Risk:** Collecting the *content* of the interaction for a "monitoring" agent is excessive if the goal is simply tracking uptime or error rates. Storing the full conversation history in a spreadsheet violates the principle that businesses should only collect and retain data "reasonably necessary and proportionate" to the purpose.
