### 2. FERPA Applicability
**Verdict: Conditionally Applicable (High Relevance)**

FERPA applies if your enterprise is an educational institution receiving federal funding or a vendor acting as a "school official" with legitimate educational interest.
*   **Why it applies here:** This agent is designed to scrape and store the *content* of interactions (`inputs` and `outputs`) between users and other agents.
*   If the agents being monitored (those listed in the "Lindy Agents" sheet) interact with students, the conversation logs generated are considered **Education Records** containing Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
*   Therefore, the automated movement and storage of these records are strictly regulated by FERPA.
