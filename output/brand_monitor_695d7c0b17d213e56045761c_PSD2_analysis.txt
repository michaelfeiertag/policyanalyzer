The **Brand Monitor** agent is designed to automate market research and brand sentiment analysis. Its specific workflow is:
1.  **Input:** Receives a Brand Name and optional keywords from a user via a web chat interface (Lindy Embed).
2.  **Processing:** Uses a "Search the Internet" tool (Browser) to scrape public news sites, social media, review platforms, and forums for mentions of the specified brand.

### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable**

**Reasoning:**
PSD2 (Directive (EU) 2015/2366) regulates **Payment Services** within the European Economic Area. It specifically applies to:
*   **Account Information Service Providers (AISPs):** Aggregating data from payment accounts (e.g., bank balances, transaction history).
*   **Payment Initiation Service Providers (PISPs):** Initiating payments on behalf of a user.
*   **Issuers of Card-Based Payment Instruments.**

The "Brand Monitor" agent:
*   **Does not** access, retrieve, or store financial account data.
*   **Does not** initiate any form of monetary transfer.
*   **Does not** request sensitive authentication data (SAD) related to banking (e.g., PINs, CVVs, bank credentials).
*   **Does not** act as an intermediary between a merchant and a bank.

Its function is strictly limited to querying **publicly available** internet data (news/social media) and internal communication. Therefore, it falls entirely outside the regulatory scope of PSD2.

### 3. Specific Violations of PSD2
**None.**

Since the agent does not perform payment services, access payment accounts, or handle payment credentials, it cannot violate the provisions of PSD2 (such as Strong Customer Authentication requirements or secure communication standards for financial APIs).
