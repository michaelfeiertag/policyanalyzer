**The ePrivacy Directive is NOT directly applicable to this agent's operation in a way that generates compliance risk.**

    1.  **Unsolicited Direct Marketing (Article 13):** Rules regarding spam via email and SMS.
    2.  **Cookies/Tracking (Article 5(3)):** Rules regarding storing information on a user's terminal equipment.
    3.  **Confidentiality & Traffic Data:** Rules for Public Electronic Communications Networks (ISPs/Telecoms).

This agent is a private, user-configured automation performing an operational task (checking a schedule) and sending functional notifications (transactional alerts) to specific, hardcoded recipients who are presumably the agent owners or colleagues.
