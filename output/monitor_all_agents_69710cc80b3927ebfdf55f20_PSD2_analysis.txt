### 1. Summary of Agent Function
The agent functions as an internal system monitor and logger. Its workflow operates as follows:
1.  **Trigger:** Runs on an hourly recurring schedule.

### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable**

PSD2 (Directive (EU) 2015/2366) applies specifically to **Payment Service Providers (PSPs)**, **Account Information Service Providers (AISPs)**, and **Payment Initiation Service Providers (PISPs)** within the European Economic Area.

This agent does not fall under the scope of PSD2 for the following reasons:
*   **No Interaction with Payment Accounts:** The agent connects exclusively to Google Workspace (Sheets) and the Lindy internal task database. It does not interface with bank APIs, credit card networks, or financial institution ledgers.
*   **No Payment Initiation:** The agent cannot initiate, authorize, or modify monetary transactions.
*   **No Account Information Aggregation:** The agent aggregates internal system logs (software debugging data), not financial transaction history or account balances from financial institutions.

### 3. Specific Violations
**None found.**

As the agent performs purely administrative and operational monitoring functions within the internal software environment and does not touch the regulated payment ecosystem, it cannot violate PSD2 standards regarding Strong Customer Authentication (SCA), Open Banking APIs, or secure communication with financial institutions.
