### 1. Summary of Agent Function
The agent, titled "Monitor All Agents," functions as an automated administrative logger. Its workflow is as follows:
1.  **Trigger:** It runs on a recurring hourly schedule.
2.  **Input:** It retrieves a list of other AI agents ("Lindy Agents") from a specific Google Sheet.
3.  **Processing:** It iterates through each agent found in the sheet, queries the Lindy system for "unread" tasks associated with that agent, and retrieves the specific details and history of those tasks.
4.  **Output:** It logs the details of these tasks into a separate Google Sheet ("Lindy Monitoring Events").
5.  **Scope:** The agent monitors software entities (other agents), not human employees.

### 2. EU AI Act Applicability
**Applicability:** **Yes.**
The agent utilizes an AI model ("md") and operates autonomously to infer inputs (task lists) and generate outputs (summarized logs). As a system deployed within an enterprise context that may affect EU data or operations, it falls under the definition of an AI System (Article 3).

**Risk Classification:** **Minimal/Limited Risk.**
*   **Prohibited Practices (Art. 5):** The agent does not employ subliminal techniques, social scoring, or biometric categorization.
*   **High-Risk AI Systems (Art. 6 & Annex III):** The agent does not fall under High-Risk categories.
    *   *Employment Context:* While Annex III lists systems used to "monitor and evaluate performance and behavior of persons" as High-Risk, this agent explicitly targets "Lindy Agents" (software bots), not humans.
    *   *Critical Infrastructure/Services:* It is a passive logging tool.
*   **Transparency (Art. 50):** As the agent does not interact directly with natural persons (it writes to a database/spreadsheet) and does not generate synthetic content intended to pass as human (it logs data), transparency obligations are minimal.
