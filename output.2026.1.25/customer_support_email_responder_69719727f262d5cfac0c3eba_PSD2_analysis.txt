### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable**

**Reasoning:**
PSD2 (Payment Services Directive 2) is a European Union regulation that applies specifically to **Payment Service Providers (PSPs)**. Its scope covers Third-Party Providers (TPPs) acting as:
*   **PISPs (Payment Initiation Service Providers):** Services that initiate payments on behalf of the user.
*   **AISPs (Account Information Service Providers):** Services that aggregate and display bank account information.

This agent operates exclusively within the domain of **communication (Gmail)** and **information retrieval (Knowledge Base)**. It does not:
*   Access bank account APIs.
*   Initiate financial transactions.
*   Aggregate financial data from banking institutions.
*   Issue payment instruments.

As the agent performs no financial functions and interacts with no payment gateways or banking infrastructure, it falls entirely outside the regulatory scope of PSD2.
