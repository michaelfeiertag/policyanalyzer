3.  **No Card Data Fields:** There are no fields in the defined schema corresponding to Credit Card Number, Expiration Date, or CVV.
4.  **No Transaction Processing:** The agent's intents (e.g., "What legal benefits...", "Compare health plans") are informational and do not involve executing financial transactions or collecting payment for services.
