**Assessment of Resume Screening Agent**

**1. Summary of Agent Function**
The "Resume Screening Agent" is an automated workflow designed to assist Human Resources. It triggers upon receiving an email in Gmail. If the email contains a resume attachment, the agent retrieves a specific Job Description from a hardcoded Google Document. It then uses an AI model to compare the resume against the job description. Based on the evaluation, it classifies the candidate as "Qualified," "Unqualified," or "Borderline" and drafts/sends a structured summary email to the user (HR/Hiring Manager) detailing the candidate's strengths and weaknesses.
