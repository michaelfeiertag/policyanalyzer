### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**PSD2 is NOT applicable to this agent.**

**Reasoning:**
The Revised Payment Services Directive (PSD2) regulates Payment Service Providers (PSPs), Third-Party Providers (TPPs), and the interaction between banks and third parties regarding payment initiation (PIS) and account information services (AIS).

To fall under the scope of PSD2, an agent must:
*   Access payment account information (e.g., bank balances, transaction history) via Open Banking APIs.
*   Initiate payment transactions.
*   Issue card-based payment instruments.
*   Act as an aggregator of financial data for customer use.

This agent strictly performs **IT system monitoring and logging**. It interacts exclusively with:

2.  **Lindy Internal API:** To read task status (metadata about the AI system's workload).

It does not interact with financial institutions, process payments, or handle sensitive authentication data (SCA) related to banking.
