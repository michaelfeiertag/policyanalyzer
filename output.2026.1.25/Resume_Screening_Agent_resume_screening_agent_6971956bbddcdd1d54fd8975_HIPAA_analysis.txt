### 1. Summary of Agent Function
The **Resume Screening Agent** is an automation tool designed to assist Human Resources or Hiring Managers. Its workflow is as follows:
1.  **Trigger:** Detects incoming emails in Gmail.
2.  **Filter:** Checks if the email contains a resume file.
3.  **Context:** Retrieves a specific Job Description from a Google Doc.
4.  **Analysis:** Compares the resume content against the job description criteria.
5.  **Output:** Sends a structured email back to the user classifying the candidate as "Qualified," "Unqualified," or "Borderline," including a summary of strengths and weaknesses.

### 2. HIPAA Applicability
**HIPAA is likely NOT applicable to this agent.**

**Reasoning:**
HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) regulates the protection of **Protected Health Information (PHI)** held by Covered Entities (healthcare providers, plans, clearinghouses) and their Business Associates.

1.  **Nature of Data:** The agent processes **resumes** and **job descriptions**. Standard resumes contain professional experience, education, and contact information. They do not contain PHI (diagnoses, treatment plans, medical histories) as defined by the Privacy Rule.
2.  **Employment Records Exception:** Even if your enterprise is a Covered Entity (e.g., a hospital), the HIPAA Privacy Rule (45 CFR ยง 160.103) explicitly **excludes** "employment records held by a covered entity in its role as employer" from the definition of PHI.
3.  **Intended Use:** The agent is explicitly defined for "hiring processes" and "candidate evaluation," which falls under Human Resources, not patient care or claims processing.
