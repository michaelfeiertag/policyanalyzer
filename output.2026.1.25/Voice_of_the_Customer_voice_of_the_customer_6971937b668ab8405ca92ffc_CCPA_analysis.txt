The agent, named "Voice of the Customer," is designed to automate the extraction of customer feedback. Its workflow is:
1.  **Trigger:** Watches the calendar of `m@mftestco.com` for **all** event starts.

4.  **Distribution:** If it was a customer call, it uses Generative AI to extract and rewrite quotes, then sends them via **Gmail** to a team distribution list (despite the description stating "Slack").
