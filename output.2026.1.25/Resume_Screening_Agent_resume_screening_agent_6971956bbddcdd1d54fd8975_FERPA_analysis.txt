### 2. FERPA Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable / Low Applicability**

FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) applies specifically to "Education Records" maintained by educational agencies or institutions (schools, universities) that receive funding from the U.S. Department of Education.

*   **Employment Records Exception:** Under FERPA regulations (34 CFR ยง 99.3), records made and maintained in relation to an individual who is employed by an educational agency or institution are **excluded** from the definition of "education records."
*   **Nature of Data:** This agent processes resumes submitted for job applications. Resumes submitted by applicants (whether they are students or external candidates) for the purpose of securing employment are classified as employment records, not education records.
*   **Enterprise Context:** If your "large enterprise" is a commercial entity (not a school), FERPA is entirely irrelevant. If your enterprise is a university, the "Employment Records" exception applies to this agent's function.
