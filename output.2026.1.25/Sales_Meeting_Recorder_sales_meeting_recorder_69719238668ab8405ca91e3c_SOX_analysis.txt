### 1. Summary of Agent Function
The **Sales Meeting Recorder** agent automates the documentation of sales prospecting calls. Its workflow is as follows:
1.  **Trigger:** Detects the start of a calendar event for a specific user (`m@mftestco.com`).
2.  **Logic:** Filters for "External" meetings only (excluding internal calls).
3.  **Action:** Joins the meeting via `LindyMeeting` to record and transcribe audio.
4.  **Action:** Creates a Google Doc in the user's "My Drive" containing the meeting notes.
5.  **Action:** Drafts a follow-up email in Gmail to the prospect summarizing the call and proposing next steps. This step **requires human confirmation** before sending.
6.  **Cleanup:** Deletes the Lindy task upon completion (or if the meeting was internal).

### 2. SOX Applicability
**Applicability: Low / Not Applicable**

SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002) primarily governs the accuracy of financial reporting, internal controls over financial data, and the retention of corporate records relevant to audits.

This agent is **not** subject to strict SOX controls for the following reasons:
*   **Nature of Data:** The agent handles preliminary sales prospecting discussions (notes and recordings). While sales eventually lead to revenue, initial sales calls and informal notes are generally not considered "financial records" or "source documents" for revenue recognition until a quote, contract, or invoice is generated.
*   **System Interaction:** The agent interacts with productivity tools (Calendar, Google Docs, Gmail) and does not interface with financial systems (ERP, General Ledger, Payroll, or Billing systems).
*   **No Financial Impact:** The agent does not execute financial transactions or modify financial data.
