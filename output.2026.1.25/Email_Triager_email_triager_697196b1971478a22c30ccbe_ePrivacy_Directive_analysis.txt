### 2. Applicability of ePrivacy Directive
The ePrivacy Directive is **applicable** to this agent.
*   **Article 5 (Confidentiality of Communications):** The agent intercepts, scans, and processes the contents of electronic communications. Under ePrivacy, storing or accessing information in the terminal equipment of a subscriber or user is regulated.
*   **Article 13 (Unsolicited Communications):** The agent possesses the capability to send automated emails (`SendToGmail`). The Directive strictly regulates the sending of automated electronic mail for the purposes of direct marketing.
