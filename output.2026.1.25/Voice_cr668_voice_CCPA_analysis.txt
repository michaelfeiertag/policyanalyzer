### 3. Specific Violations of CCPA
Based on the `[AGENT_DEFINITION]`, the following definitive violations and significant risks are identified:

**Violation A: Failure to Provide Notice at Collection (CCPA ยง 1798.100)**
*   **Evidence:** In the `Conversation Start` topic, the agent sends a greeting: *"Hello and thank you for calling {System.Bot.Name}. Please note that some responses are generated by AI and may require verification for accuracy..."*
*   **The Violation:** CCPA requires a business to inform consumers *at or before the point of collection* as to the categories of personal information to be collected and the purposes for which they will be used. *   **Significance:** High. This is a statutory violation at the entry point of the conversation.

*   **The Violation:** While CCPA focuses on privacy, it requires businesses to be transparent about data practices. *   **Significance:** Critical. This instruction overrides potential safety filters and prevents the agent from providing accurate compliance information required by law.

3.  **Implement a Privacy Topic:** Create a new Topic (e.g., `PrivacyPolicy`) that triggers on phrases like "Privacy policy," "Delete my data," or "How do you use my info." This topic should provide the required disclosures or a direct path to a human agent specifically for compliance requests.
4.  **Data Minimization:** Review the `Lesson 3` topic. Ensure the collection of "State" is strictly necessary for the shipping calculation and is not retained longer than necessary if the user does not complete the purchase.
