### 1. Summary of the Agent's Function
The "Email Follow-up Reminder" agent acts as a productivity workflow automation tool. It monitors the user's sent emails via Gmail. If the agent determines via LLM logic that a specific email requires a response, it initiates a 3-day (4320 minutes) timer. If no response is detected from the recipient after this duration, the agent drafts a follow-up email in the user's draft folder.

The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) regulates the collection, dissemination, and use of consumer information, specifically regarding consumer credit information used for credit, employment, insurance, or housing eligibility determinations.

To fall under the scope of FCRA, an entity or agent generally must:
1.  Act as a Consumer Reporting Agency (CRA) by assembling or evaluating consumer credit information for the purpose of furnishing consumer reports to third parties.
2.  Be a "furnisher" of information to CRAs.
3.  Be a "user" of consumer reports to take adverse action against a consumer.

It operates solely on the user's personal email metadata (timestamps and reply status) and content for the purpose of workflow management.
