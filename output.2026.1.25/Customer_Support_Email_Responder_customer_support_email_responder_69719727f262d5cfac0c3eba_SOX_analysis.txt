The agent is designed to automate the drafting of responses to incoming customer support emails via Gmail. ### 2. SOX Applicability
**Determination: Not Applicable**

**Reasoning:**
The Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) specifically governs the integrity of financial reporting, internal controls over financial data, and the prevention of corporate accounting fraud.
*   **No Financial Data Access:** This agent connects strictly to Gmail and a generic Knowledge Base. It does not have access to Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, General Ledgers, Payroll, or financial databases (e.g., SAP, Oracle Financials, NetSuite).
*   **Nature of Operations:** The agent functions within the domain of Customer Support, not Finance or Accounting. While customer support interactions can theoretically impact revenue recognition (e.g., disputes), the agent acts as a drafting tool, not a system of record for financial transactions.
*   **No Impact on ICFR:** The agent does not alter, delete, or generate data that would impact Internal Control over Financial Reporting (ICFR).
