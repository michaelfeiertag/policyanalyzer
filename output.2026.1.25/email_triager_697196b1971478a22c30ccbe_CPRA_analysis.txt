### 1. Summary of Agent Function
The "Email Triager" agent is an automated workflow designed to monitor an inbox (Gmail). Upon receiving a message, it utilizes a Large Language Model (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) to analyze the email content and categorize it into one of five streams:
1.  **High Priority:** Labels as "Important".
2.  **Customer Question:** Labels as "Requires Response".
3.  **Newsletter:** Labels as "Newsletter".
4.  **Spam/Sales:** Labels as "Spam".
5.  **Customer FAQ:** Labels as "Customer FAQ", searches a connected Knowledge Base, and automatically sends an email reply based on the search results.

*   **Automated Decision Making (ADM):** The agent uses AI to profile incoming communications and make automated decisions regarding their disposition (labeling) and response (auto-replying), which falls under CPRA provisions regarding automated processing.
