The agent is **Non-Compliant** due to two significant configuration violations found in the `GptComponentMetadata` and `AISettings`.

This makes the agent highly susceptible to jailbreaking, prompt injection, and the generation of harmful content (e.g., hate speech, malware generation instructions) if a user pivots the conversation away from the website content. **Violation 2: Failure of Validity and Reliability (Map 1.5, Govern 1.2)**

The agent will confidently answer questions about competitors, unrelated topics, or outdated information contained in the LLM's base model, rather than restricting itself to the verified compliance data on the target website. It breaks the "Grounding" required for a trustworthy enterprise agent.
