### 2. COPPA Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable / Low Relevance**

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) applies to operators of commercial websites and online services that are:
1.  Directed to children under the age of 13.
2.  General audience sites where the operator has "actual knowledge" that they are collecting personal information from a child under 13.

**Analysis:**
*   **Target Audience:** This agent is explicitly designed for **employment and hiring**. The labor market is legally restricted in most jurisdictions to individuals over 14 (often 16-18 for professional roles). The content (resumes, job descriptions, professional skills) is not directed at children under 13.
*   **Actual Knowledge:** The agent does not include age-gating questions, nor does it request dates of birth in a structured format. There is no mechanism in the agent's logic that would solicit data from a child, nor is there reason to believe children under 13 would be submitting resumes for professional employment.
