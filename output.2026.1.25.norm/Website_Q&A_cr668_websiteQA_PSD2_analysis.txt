### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**PSD2 is NOT Applicable.**

The Second Payment Services Directive (PSD2) regulates payment services within the EU/EEA. It specifically targets:
*   **Payment Initiation Services (PIS):** Initiating a payment order at the request of the payment service user with respect to a payment account held at another payment service provider.
*   **Account Information Services (AIS):** Providing consolidated information on one or more payment accounts held by the payment service user with either another payment service provider or with more than one payment service provider.

**Reasoning:**
*   **Nature of Service:** The agent is defined purely as an informational retrieval tool ("Website Q&A"). It parses public web content to answer general questions.
*   **No Financial Data Access:** There is no code, API connection, or plugin defined in the `components` or `knowledgeSources` that connects to banking infrastructure (ASPSPs).
*   **No Transactional Capability:** The agent lacks the logic to execute, authorize, or process financial transfers or payments.
