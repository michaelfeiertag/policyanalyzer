### 2. COPPA Applicability
**Verdict: Not Applicable / Low Relevance**

**Reasoning:**
COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) applies to operators of websites or online services that are either:
1.  **Directed to children** under the age of 13.
2.  General audience services where the operator has **"actual knowledge"** they are collecting personal information from a child under 13.

The knowledge source defined in the agent is `https://microsoft.com`. This is a general audience commercial enterprise site, not a site directed specifically toward children. Furthermore, the agent's instructions ("Maintain a polite and professional tone") and topics suggest a general customer service utility.
