### 2. PSD2 Applicability
**PSD2 is NOT Applicable.**

**Reasoning:**
The Second Payment Services Directive (PSD2) regulates **Payment Initiation Service Providers (PISP)** and **Account Information Service Providers (AISP)** within the European Economic Area. Its scope is strictly limited to:
1.  Initiating payment transactions on behalf of a user.
2.  Aggregating and displaying information from **payment accounts** (e.g., checking accounts, credit cards) held at third-party financial institutions (banks) via Open Banking APIs.

The "Financial Insights" agent acts as an internal knowledge management tool and market research assistant.
*   It does not connect to banking APIs to retrieve customer transaction history or balance data (AISP).
*   It does not provide functionality to transfer funds or pay bills (PISP).
*   The "financial data" it retrieves is defined in the manifest as "analyst reports," "sales figures from internal databases," and "market analysis reports." This constitutes corporate business intelligence and proprietary data, not regulated payment account data.
