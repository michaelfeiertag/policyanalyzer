### 2. ECPA Applicability
**Yes, ECPA is applicable.**
The Electronic Communications Privacy Act (18 U.S.C. §§ 2510-2523) governs the interception of wire, oral, and electronic communications and the access to stored wire and electronic communications. As this agent operates on enterprise communication channels (Teams, Copilot) and processes, receives, and replies to electronic text messages, it falls under the purview of "electronic communications."

However, for a violation to occur, the agent would generally need to:
1.  Intercept communications to which it is not a party (wiretapping).
2.  Disclose contents of communications to unauthorized third parties without consent.
3.  Access stored communications without authorization.
