The agent ("Email Triager") is an automated workflow integrated with Gmail. Its specific functions are:

**Yes, ECPA is applicable.**
The Electronic Communications Privacy Act (18 U.S.C. §§ 2510-2523) and the Stored Communications Act (18 U.S.C. §§ 2701-2712) apply because this agent:
1.  **Accesses Electronic Communications:** It reads the contents of emails stored on the service provider's system (Gmail).
2.  **Intercepts/Processes Traffic:** It acts on messages immediately upon receipt.
3.  **Alters Communications:** It modifies metadata (labels) and generates new electronic communications (replies).
