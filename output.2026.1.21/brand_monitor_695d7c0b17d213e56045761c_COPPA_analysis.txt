### 2. COPPA Applicability
**COPPA is NOT Applicable.**

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) applies to:
1.  Websites or online services directed to children under 13.
2.  General audience sites/services where the operator has "actual knowledge" they are collecting personal information from a child under 13.

**Reasoning:**
*   **Subject Matter:** The agent is explicitly designed for commercial "Brand Monitoring," "Report Generation," and "Market Sentiment." These are professional business activities.
*   **Target Audience:** The prompt engineering ("Your Weekly Business Insights," "Professional closing") indicates the target audience is adults/business professionals.
*   **No "Kid-Appeal":** The agent does not feature cartoons, games, child-oriented language, or incentives that would attract children under 13.
*   **General Audience:** As a general audience tool (business utility), COPPA only applies if you knowingly collect data from a child. There is no mechanism in this agent that invites a child to self-identify.
