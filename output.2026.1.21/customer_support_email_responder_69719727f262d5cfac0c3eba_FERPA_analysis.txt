### 1. Summary of Agent Function
**Agent:** Customer Support Email Responder (`customer-support-email-responder`)
**Identity:** `m@mftestco.com`
**Function:** The agent monitors a Gmail inbox for incoming messages. Upon receipt, it searches a connected Knowledge Base. If an answer is found, it uses the **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** language model to draft a response.
**Key Behavior:** The agent is configured to **draft** replies (`saveAsDraft: true`) rather than send them automatically, ensuring a human-in-the-loop workflow.

### 2. FERPA Applicability
**Verdict:** **NOT APPLICABLE**

**Reasoning:**
*   **Regulatory Scope:** FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) applies specifically to "Educational Agencies or Institutions" that receive funds from the U.S. Department of Education. It protects "Education Records" (grades, transcripts, disciplinary records).
*   **Agent Context:** The agent definition identifies the operator as `mftestco.com` and the function as "Customer Support." These identifiers indicate a commercial enterprise context rather than an educational institution.
*   **Data Type:** "Customer inquiries" are distinct from "Education Records." Unless this agent is specifically handling student transcripts or academic records on behalf of a university, FERPA regulations do not apply to this workflow.

### 3. Specific Violations
Based on the determination that FERPA is **Not Applicable**, there are **no FERPA violations**.

*However, purely from a **General Security & Governance** perspective, the following critical risk is identified:*

**Critical Risk: Violation of Least Privilege (Scope Creep)**
*   **Issue:** The agent's Google Authentication configuration grants it excessively broad permissions that are not utilized by its logic flow.
*   **Details:** The agent's logic only interacts with **Gmail**. However, the `auth` object (`OAuth`) grants valid tokens for:
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive` (Full Read/Write access to Google Drive)
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents` (Google Docs)
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets`
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar`
*   **Risk:** This creates a significant "blast radius." If this agent's credentials were compromised, an attacker would have full access to the organization's files and documents, not just the customer support emails.

### 4. Recommendations for Remediation
While FERPA remediation is not required, the following actions are necessary to reduce practical organizational risk:

1.  **Restrict Authentication Scopes:** Re-authenticate the agent with a strictly scoped token. Remove permissions for Drive, Docs, Spreadsheets, and Calendar. The agent should **only** hold scopes for:
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly`
    *   `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.compose`
2.  **Maintain "Draft" Mode:** Ensure the `saveAsDraft: true` setting in the `ActionNode` (Respond to emailer) remains locked. This "Human in the Loop" setup effectively mitigates risks associated with AI hallucinations or incorrect policy advice.

### 5. Overall Compliance Rating
**Rating:** **COMPLIANT** (FERPA)
